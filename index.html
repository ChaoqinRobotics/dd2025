About the Workshop

As autonomous systems become increasingly integral across different areas, the demand for advanced control and trajectory optimization techniques for real-world deployment has become more essential. Data-driven techniques have merged in recent years, providing a powerful tool to push the boundaries of precision and adaptability beyond the limits of traditional systems.

The workshop will focus on the integration of machine learning, theory of optimization and control, and real-world data to improve the performance and adaptability of autonomous systems. Participants will explore the use of datasets for refining control algorithms, developing predictive models, and optimizing trajectories in unpredictable conditions.
